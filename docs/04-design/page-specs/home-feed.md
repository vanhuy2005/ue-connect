# home-feed.md

## 1. Purpose
Define the main feed surface where UEers see updates, posts, and connection prompts.

## 2. Design Decision
Use a card-based feed with clear hierarchy, safe spacing, and trusted social cues.

## 3. Rationale
The home feed must feel active and social without becoming visually noisy.

## 4. Rules
- Prioritize primary content first.
- Keep post metadata readable.
- Use consistent feed card structure.

## 5. Do / Don't
Do: make scanning easy.

Don't: bury content under too many badges or actions.

## 6. Tokens / Specs
Feed cards should use white surfaces, soft borders, modest shadows, and 16px content rhythm.

## 7. Component / Screen Impact
Impacts feed cards, reaction row, avatars, badges, empty states, and inline actions.

## 8. QA Checklist
- Content hierarchy is easy to scan.
- Feed actions are obvious but not overwhelming.
- Mobile cards remain readable.

## 9. AI Prompt Notes
Ask for a clean social feed that feels polished and student-first, not a generic dashboard.
