# clubs.md

## 1. Purpose
Describe the club discovery, joining, and club profile experience.

## 2. Design Decision
Show clubs as community hubs with distinct identity, activity, and trust signals.

## 3. Rationale
Clubs need enough personality to feel alive without becoming chaotic.

## 4. Rules
- Show club identity clearly.
- Include membership or activity state.
- Keep discovery and detail views visually connected.

## 5. Do / Don't
Do: make clubs feel lively and organized.

Don't: overload the page with banners and mismatched visuals.

## 6. Tokens / Specs
Use club cards, badges, media, and structured content sections.

## 7. Component / Screen Impact
Impacts club cards, join states, member counts, events, and announcements.

## 8. QA Checklist
- Club identity is easy to scan.
- Join state is clear.
- The screen works for both discovery and detail.

## 9. AI Prompt Notes
Ask for a club surface that feels community-led and polished.
