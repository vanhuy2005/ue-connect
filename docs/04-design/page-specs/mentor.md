# mentor.md

## 1. Purpose
Describe the mentor discovery and matching experience.

## 2. Design Decision
Present mentors as trustworthy experts with approachable personality and clear match signals.

## 3. Rationale
Mentor surfaces must support confidence, not just popularity.

## 4. Rules
- Prioritize expertise, availability, and trust cues.
- Keep matching criteria understandable.
- Avoid over-selling the surface.

## 5. Do / Don't
Do: make mentorship feel supportive and concrete.

Don't: make mentor cards look like generic marketplace listings.

## 6. Tokens / Specs
Use mentor badges, profile cards, schedules, and a reserved accent color like purple only where needed.

## 7. Component / Screen Impact
Impacts mentor cards, filters, availability indicators, booking actions, and bio areas.

## 8. QA Checklist
- Mentor trust cues are visible.
- Availability is easy to read.
- Primary mentor actions are obvious.

## 9. AI Prompt Notes
Ask for a trusted mentor directory with calm, academic credibility.
