# desktop-feed-wireframe.md

## 1. Purpose
Capture the desktop feed composition and annotation notes.

## 2. Design Decision
Use a wider content area with side support or secondary rails where needed.

## 3. Rationale
Desktop should expand the feed without turning it into a stretched mobile layout.

## 4. Rules
- Keep the main content width controlled.
- Secondary panels must not overpower the feed.
- Maintain a clear hierarchy between primary and secondary content.

## 5. Do / Don't
Do: preserve a premium reading rhythm.

Don't: let the feed span too wide across the screen.

## 6. Tokens / Specs
Use desktop container widths, clear column gaps, and comfortable whitespace.

## 7. Component / Screen Impact
Impacts top nav, feed column, side rails, and desktop card sizing.

## 8. QA Checklist
- Content width is comfortable to read.
- The layout does not feel empty.
- Actions remain clearly visible.

## 9. AI Prompt Notes
Ask AI for a desktop feed with strong hierarchy and restrained width.
