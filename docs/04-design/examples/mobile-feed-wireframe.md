# mobile-feed-wireframe.md

## 1. Purpose
Capture the mobile feed composition and annotation notes.

## 2. Design Decision
Use a one-column feed with visible action affordances and compact metadata.

## 3. Rationale
Mobile is the default experience, so the wireframe must prioritize thumb reach and scanability.

## 4. Rules
- Keep the primary content centered in the viewport.
- Preserve comfortable card spacing.
- Keep feed actions reachable by thumb.

## 5. Do / Don't
Do: annotate interactions that matter on small screens.

Don't: overload the wireframe with desktop-only patterns.

## 6. Tokens / Specs
Use single-column spacing, card padding, and bottom-nav-safe layout assumptions.

## 7. Component / Screen Impact
Impacts feed cards, reaction row, avatar size, and mobile navigation.

## 8. QA Checklist
- The feed is easy to scan on a phone.
- Actions are reachable.
- No card feels too dense.

## 9. AI Prompt Notes
Ask AI to show a mobile feed wireframe that still feels polished and social.
